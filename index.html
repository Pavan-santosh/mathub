MAT-HUB

MATERIALS HUB

01.REGISTRATION


General Consumer needs to register by providing the basic information like Mobile number or email id

02.ONLINE BOOKING


Registered consumer needS to login with credentials for online booking. Search the delivery address through Google map and should fill the mandatory details like district, Mandal, Ward for Door Delivery. Vehicle Type, Quantity, Reach/ Depot selection and delivery location to be mentioned and confirmed before proceeding to secured payment Gateway.

03.ONLINE PAYMENTS


Based on the delivery location confirmation, Consumer can complete the payment process of Sand and Transportation Cost through the portal by selecting payment methods (Debit/ Credit Cards/ Net Banking/ UPI etc.) Once payment is processed, order receipt will be generated.

04.MATERIALS LOADING


Our In-charge at Reach/ Depot will verify the loaded quantity for the vehicle and enable GPS navigation will be enabled to the consumer for further tracking. Consumer will receive Kata-Slip, Invoice, and Sand way Bill at time of Sand Delivery.

05.ORDER TRACKING


Consumers will be notified about dispatch and delivery status for orders booked. Consumer can track the order through our web portal.

06.DOOR DELIVERY


Dispatch will be processed by us to the delivery location specified by the consumer only. Consumer need to confirm the receipt of delivery of sand through automated IVRS call received by the consumer after delivery. NO EXTRA charge will be collected from the Consumer after successful Delivery.
